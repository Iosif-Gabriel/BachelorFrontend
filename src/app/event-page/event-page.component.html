About this space

A beautiful treehouse, in the middle of nature. It overlooks a small pond which is full of fish and there's also a nearby river which you can hear from the porch, that adds to the beautiful nature scene. The space The place (Eagles' Nest) is situated in Moisei in the Nature Park Muntii Maramuresului, 20 km away from the Borsa ski Slope, 12 km from the steam train in Viseu. It has a porch and a hammock where you can have a great up of coffee in the middle of nature. It can host up to 4 guests. Guest access The place is 300m from the main road, easily accessible and has many parking spots. Being not in the main road it is also very quiet.
